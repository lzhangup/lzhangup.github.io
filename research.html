Research Interests

His research interests include model predictive control, reinforcement learning, and system modelling, with applications to energy systems, electric connected vehicles, smart cities, demand side management, and demand response.

Projects

Projects led as principal investigator

  • 2021-2023: Modelling and intelligent control of battery-supercapacitor hybrid energy storage systems in electric vehicles funded by the Royal Academy of Engineering, UK.

  • 2018-2020: Energy management and control of electric vehicles funded by the National Research Foundation (NRF) of South Africa.

  • 2018-2020: Design and maintenance optimisation for large-scale PV arrays funded by the Royal Academy of Engineering, UK.

  • 2014–2019: Junior Chair on Energy Efficiency for industrial processes funded by Exxaro Resources Ltd.

  • 2018–2019: Train energy and dynamic simulation funded by Transnet SOC Ltd.

  • 2017–2019: Research Development Programme Fund from the University of Pretoria.

  • 2017–2017: Knowledge Interchange and Collaboration funding from the National Research Foundation (NRF) of South Africa.

Projects participated

  • 2015–2018: Modelling, optimization and control for industrial energy efficiency and performance evaluation, NRF THRIP & Industry project.

  • 2015–2018: Modelling and control system approaches to building energy efficiency optimisation, NRF.

  • 2015–2017: Energy efficiency operation optimisation of water resources in a dam river system, NRF South Africa/MOST China joint science and technology research collaboration project.

  • 2013–2016: Cooperative and active fault-tolerant control of power systems with multiple renewable resources, NRF South Africa/MOST China joint science and technology research collaboration project.

  • 2010–2012: Energy efficient operation control of heavy haul trains, National Natural Science Foundation, China.
